Pedagogy

My pedagogical approach offers students opportunities to create multimodal, interactive writing. I especially focus on encouraging students to "show what they know" in alternate ways besides the tradiitonal, lnear research essay. My approach emphasizes hands-on learning, collaborative research, and the integration of digital tools with traditional humanistic inquiry and writing methods.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in creating inclusive learning environments where students engage directly with composition and digital methods while developing critical thinking skills about technology's role in written expression.

  • Experiential learning through digital projects
  • Collaborative knowledge creation
  • Critical evaluation of digital methodologies
  • Interdisciplinary approaches to humanities questions

Digital Literacy Initiatives

Developing programs that bridge the gap between traditional humanities education and emerging digital technologies.

  • Faculty development workshops
  • Student research mentorship programs
  • Community outreach and public engagement
  • Curriculum design and assessment

Innovative Methods

Implementing cutting-edge pedagogical techniques that enhance student engagement and learning outcomes.

  • Project-based learning with real-world applications
  • Flipped classroom techniques
  • Peer review and collaborative assessment
  • Integration of AI and machine learning tools
